Lecturer
Cori Cohen, RDN

Cori Cohen is a certified Registered Dietitian Nutritionist practicing in New York City. Ms. Cohen is a strong believer in the healing power of food and the undeniable connection between lifestyle choices and optimal wellness. She emphasizes customized nutrition, mindfulness, meditation and purposeful movement as part of a healthy balanced lifestyle.

Over the span of her career Ms. Cohen has counseled a large and diverse population of individuals. She has helped many reach their weight and body composition goals, improve memory, find more energy to enjoy their lives, significantly improve blood sugar and cardiac markers, manage IBS and IBD, navigate life with Celiac disease, and improve overall health by focusing on behavioral changes. In addition to her private practice, Ms. Cohen leads seminars and workshops on a variety of health and nutrition topics.
