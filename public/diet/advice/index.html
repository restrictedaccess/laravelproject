Behavior Change Tips

1. Personal mantras can help motivate and inspire you to be your best. Create a positive and powerful statement to help you reach your goals. Example: I can and I will.

2. Setting goals can be a powerful tool to inspire change. Remember, small changes lead to big ones.

3. Finding it tough to achieve your goals? Try to find a "keystone habit". Keystone habits have a very powerful ripple effect in your life, helping you to reach your goals . Ex: When I exercise in the morning, I tend to eat healthy all day. When I eat healthy, I have more energy and perform better at work. This decreases work stress, helping me to sleep better. And so on.....

4. Build an environment that allows good habits to thrive. A few examples: Try to avoid driving by your favorite fast food restaurant, especially when you are hungry. Join a gym that has multiple locations making getting in your exercise even easier. Keep healthy stacks stocked in your purse or car if your schedule throws you a lot of curve balls that tend to mess up your nutrition goals.

5. Going out to eat? Try eating an apple on the way. This helps to take the hunger "edge" off, helping you make better choices once you arrive.
